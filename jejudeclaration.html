Jeju Declaration (CECAR10, 2025 – Jeju, Korea)

The Jeju Declaration reaffirms the civil engineering community’s commitment to sustainability, inclusivity, and innovation. It highlights the transformative role of artificial intelligence (AI), big data, and smart technologies in shaping the next generation of infrastructure and civil engineering education.

AI and Smart Technologies in Civil Engineering

The declaration identifies AI, IoT, and data analytics as key tools for improving design, construction, and maintenance of infrastructure. These technologies enable the creation of intelligent, adaptive, and sustainable systems that interact with users and the environment in real time.

Education and Professional Development

It emphasizes the need to integrate AI and smart technologies into engineering curricula and professional training. Continuous learning and digital literacy are essential for engineers to lead in developing smart and resilient urban environments.

Overall Vision

Together, these commitments establish a forward-looking framework—advancing civil engineering as a human-centered, technology-enabled, and sustainability-driven profession.
